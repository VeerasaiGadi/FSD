Services

Aditya Engineering College offers a wide range of services to its students, including:
Academics: The college offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering, technology, and management. The curriculum is designed to provide students with the knowledge and skills they need to succeed in their chosen field.

Placements: The college has a strong placement record, with students receiving offers from leading companies in India and abroad. The college offers a variety of placement services, including career counseling, mock interviews, and resume writing workshops.

Research: The college encourages research and innovation among its students. The college has a number of research labs, and students are encouraged to participate in research projects.

Co-curricular activities: The college offers a variety of co-curricular activities, including sports, cultural events, and student government. These activities help students develop their leadership and teamwork skills.

Infrastructure: The college has a state-of-the-art infrastructure, including well-equipped classrooms, laboratories, and libraries. The college also has a hostel facility for students.
In addition to these services, Aditya Engineering College also offers a number of other services, such as counseling, financial aid, and alumni support.

Academic support: The college offers a variety of academic support services, including tutorial programs, remedial classes, and career counseling.
